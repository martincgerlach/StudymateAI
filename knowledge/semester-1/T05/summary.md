# T05 Summary: Content Production, Visual Branding, AI Images, Photoshop, SCRUM, and Teamwork

## Main Focus

T05 introduced content production and project work with visual branding, AI images, Photoshop, research, SCRUM, and teamwork.

The material focuses on:

- Pre-production.
- Production.
- Post-production.
- Smartphone photo/video production.
- Visual branding strategy.
- AI image generation.
- Photoshop and AI tools.
- Storyboards and still images.
- SCRUM planning.
- Team collaboration.
- Reusing skills from earlier themes.

## Content Production

The theme introduces basic content production as a process:

- Pre-production: planning, concept, research, storyboard.
- Production: creating photo, video, graphics, or other content.
- Post-production: editing, exporting, publishing, and improving.

The theme also connects content production to redesigning a company website.

## Visual Branding

Visual branding is about creating a consistent visual identity.

Important areas:

- Brand personality.
- Visual style.
- Tone.
- Colors.
- Typography.
- Image style.
- Consistency across touchpoints.

## AI and Image Work

AI image tools can support idea development and visual production, but the designer still needs to guide the result.

Important ideas:

- AI tools are useful for visualizing ideas.
- Good prompts matter.
- AI output still needs critique, selection, and editing.
- The result should match the design purpose.

## SCRUM and Project Management

SCRUM is presented as a practical toolbox, not a strict system that must always be followed perfectly.

Useful concepts:

- Product owner.
- Scrum master.
- Development team.
- Product backlog.
- User stories.
- Scrum board.
- Tasks.
- Sprint.
- Daily scrum.

For student projects, the point is to use the parts that help planning and progress.

## Useful Exam Explanation

I can explain how content production moves from planning to production to editing. I can also explain how SCRUM helped organize tasks, roles, priorities, and deadlines in a group project.
