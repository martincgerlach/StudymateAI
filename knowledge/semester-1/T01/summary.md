# T01 Summary: Intro Project, Research, Creativity, and Title Sequence

## Main Focus

T01 introduced project work, research, creative problem solving, and group collaboration through a title sequence task.

The material focuses on:

- Research before idea development.
- Analysing an existing title intro.
- Understanding framing, camera movement, perspective, duration, sound, and action.
- Producing a short title sequence with own material or properly credited assets.
- Understanding different creative working styles.
- Using creativity as a combination of knowledge, imagination, and evaluation.

## Key Methods

### Research Before Production

The process starts with research, then idea development, then execution.

For a title sequence, the research can include watching an existing intro and noting:

- Shot type.
- Camera movement.
- Perspective.
- Duration.
- Sound.
- Action.
- Mood and genre.

### Creative Problem Solving

The Basadur material introduces the idea that people contribute differently in creative work.

This is useful for teamwork because group members may have different strengths in:

- Generating ideas.
- Structuring ideas.
- Evaluating ideas.
- Executing ideas.

## Useful Exam Explanation

I can explain that I did not start by designing immediately. I first researched the format and analysed how existing title sequences use visuals, timing, sound, and atmosphere. That gave me a better foundation for my own decisions.
